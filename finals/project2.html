Light & Dark

This project is a digital adaptation of Jorge Luis Borges’ “His End And His Beggining”. Using a combination of HTML, CSS & Javascript, the three paragraphs within the story is cut up into three chapters: nightmare, truth and realization, themes that can be found throughout.
