Interactive Story

This project is a digital adaptation of Jorge Luis Borges’ “His End And His Beginning”. It breaks down the paragraphs into three different chapters: nightmare, truth & realization. These chapters are then made into different pages so viewers are able to read them separately or all together. The website itself is interactive and consists of animations, hover states and quiery input.
